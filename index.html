Who we are

The Group of Companies specializes in the construction and fit out of large projects. We are confident that we
can meet the needs of any customer to the highest quality.The Jasaf Group is part of FAMCORP whic is a
holding company founded in 1975 that operates internationally.

The Group is made up of three main Companies : Jasaf Building Technology Company LLC,Jasaf Joinery
and Jasaf Electromechanical Company (JEMCO) LLC.
